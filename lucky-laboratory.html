💡 Health Tips

Expert advice for better health

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration helps in accurate blood test results and overall health.

🥗 Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your diet. Regular health checkups help detect deficiencies early.

🏃 Regular Exercise

30 minutes of daily exercise improves blood circulation and maintains healthy cholesterol levels.

😴 Quality Sleep

Get 7-8 hours of sleep daily. Good sleep is essential for maintaining hormone balance and immune system.
